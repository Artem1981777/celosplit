CeloSplit Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

1. Data We Collect

CeloSplit does not collect or store any personal data on our servers. All data (groups, expenses) is stored locally in your browser.

2. Blockchain Data

Transactions you initiate are recorded on the public Celo blockchain. This is inherent to blockchain technology and outside our control.

3. No Tracking

We do not use cookies, analytics, or any tracking technologies.

4. Wallet Address

Your wallet address is provided by MiniPay and used only to display balances and process transactions.
